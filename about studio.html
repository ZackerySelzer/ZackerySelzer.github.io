ABOUT OUR STUDIO

In our creative studio we create our jewelry over open fire of around 1200 degreees. Each and every piece of jewerly is unique and is created by veterans in this studio. We work from the fundaments of old nordic traditions which is originally from the bronzeage around 1700.

The lace we use originally comes from parachute lines that follows the high quality spedifications. In addition every armband is made at this site and is under serious quality control and by engaged veterans. The are 648 choice bracelets. They come in 9 different figures, 6 different colors, 4 materials, and comes in 3 lengths, 17cm, 19 cm, and 21 cm. If you need another length, you can send us an email or call. We will always find a solution.

We are proud at we do make a difference. GOVESU is created with the the heart and the projects are the most important for us. Therefore it is natural that only veterans that work in our production and creative place. With will let people and handwork go hand in hand. We take into consideration the veterans desire and abilities- Some work part-time while other can choose to work fulltime if they desire. No matter what, we are together ...
